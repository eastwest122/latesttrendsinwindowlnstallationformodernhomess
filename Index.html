2. Types of Windows & Their Advantages

a. Double Hung Windows

Classic and practical, these feature two vertically sliding sashes. Ideal for ventilation control—lower sash for fresh air, sash top for warm air escape.

. Use of quality materials: vinyl, aluminum, or custom as needed

c. Sliding Windows

Horizontally gliding sashes—streamlined, space-saving, budget-friendly, and great for wide openings .

d. Bay Windows

Three-pane projections (usually a center picture flanked by operable casement or double hung), that expand space and light. Often built into seating areas or showrooms.

e. Bow Windows

Gentle curves formed by four to six panels, offering panoramic views, more natural light, and a graceful design.

f. Vinyl Windows

Affordable, low-maintenance frames with excellent insulation and a long lifespan (25–50 years).
